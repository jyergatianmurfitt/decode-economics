About

I'm a London-based, aspiring fron-end engineer. My development journey began at the beginning of 2020 when I developed a WordPress website for a business idea I had. I couldn't afford to hire a developer so I taught myself how to develop it. The WordPress interface is a great tool for a beginner and I augmented my knowledge by learning CSS and HTML.

I spent the next year building and tweaking my website and loving every moment of creating and molding something digital. I realised that this is the industry I want to develop a career in; I love that it's constantly improving, powerfully scaleable and I can use my History of Art degree to aide my attention to detail. For the last 3 months I have been teaching myself JavaScript via FreeCodeCamp, Udemy and other online tutorials and I look forward to continue working on learning this language and moving on to JavaScript libraries.
